# Taskflow PRD (v1.0)
*Your entire life, in one place — guided by AI.*

---

## 1) One-Liner

Taskflow is an AI-powered self-improvement OS that studies your behavior, plans your life, and guides you toward becoming the best version of yourself — replacing every scattered productivity app with one intelligent hub.

---

## 2) The Problem

People who want to improve their lives are forced to juggle:
- A separate task app
- A separate habit tracker
- A separate notes/journal app
- A separate calendar
- A separate accountability tool (NoFap, screen time, etc.)
- Generic AI tools that don't know anything about them

The result? Overwhelm, inconsistency, and zero progress.

There is no single tool that **knows you**, **tracks everything**, and **actively guides you** toward a better life.

---

## 3) The Solution

Taskflow puts everything in one place:

- **Tasks** — daily tasks, goals, to-dos
- **Habits** — build and track positive habits
- **Accountability** — NoFap/NoPorn tracker, streaks, resets
- **Notes & Journal** — capture thoughts, reflect on your day
- **Calendar** — plan your week and month
- **AI Brain** — studies ALL of the above and guides you with personalized suggestions, plans, and insights

The AI doesn't just respond to what you type — it **learns your patterns** over time and proactively helps you improve.

---

## 4) Primary Users (MVP Focus)

- High school and college students trying to get their life together
- Young builders (creators, coders, entrepreneurs) who want structure
- People struggling with discipline, procrastination, or bad habits
- Anyone who uses 5+ apps for self-improvement and wants it in one place

## 6) Core Features (Full Vision)

### 🏠 Dashboard (Home)
- Overview of your day at a glance
- AI daily briefing: "Here's what matters today"
- Progress summary: streaks, completed tasks, mood
- Quick actions: Add task, log habit, open journal

### ✅ Tasks
- Create tasks with title, due date, priority, notes
- AI suggests task priorities based on your patterns
- Daily task planning: "What should I focus on today?"
- Recurring tasks, subtasks, lists/categories

### 🔁 Habits
- Create and track daily/weekly habits
- Streak tracking with visual progress
- AI detects which habits you're struggling with and suggests adjustments
- Habit stacking suggestions ("After you work out, do X")

### 🚫 Accountability (NoFap / NoPorn / Custom)
- Dedicated accountability tracker separate from habits
- Day counter with streak and reset logging
- Reflection prompt on relapse: "What triggered this? How do we prevent it?"
- AI provides encouragement and pattern analysis over time
- Custom accountability challenges (screen time, junk food, etc.)

### 📓 Notes & Journal
- General notes (organized by folders/tags)
- Daily journal with optional AI prompt: "What went well today?"
- AI reads journal entries over time and surfaces patterns:
  - "You tend to be more productive on days you journal in the morning"
  - "You mentioned feeling unmotivated 4 times this week — here's what might help"

### 📅 Calendar
- Weekly and monthly view
- Connect tasks and habits to specific dates
- AI suggests scheduling based on your best work times and energy patterns

### 🤖 AI Brain (Core Differentiator)
The AI studies everything the user feeds into Taskflow:
- Task completion rates
- Habit streaks and failures
- Journal entries and tone
- Time of day patterns
- Accountability resets

And uses it to:
- Generate a personalized daily plan ("Plan My Day")
- Surface insights: "You complete more tasks on days you exercise"
- Suggest better outcomes: "Based on your patterns, moving your deep work to morning could help"
- Give proactive nudges: "You haven't journaled in 3 days — want to do a quick check-in?"
- Be a coach, not just a tool

## 7) MVP Scope (What Ships First)

MVP is complete when a user can:

- [ ] Sign up, log in, log out
- [ ] Use the Dashboard home page
- [ ] Create, complete, and delete tasks
- [ ] Create a habit and mark it done today with streak tracking
- [ ] Use the AI "Plan My Day" feature
- [ ] Write a basic journal/note entry
- [ ] View a basic calendar

**MVP AI feature (locked): Plan My Day**
- Uses tasks + habits + available time
- Outputs: top 3 priorities + time blocks + habit slots + fallback plan
- Plan is saveable and viewable later the same day

---

## 8) Non-Goals for MVP

- Social/community features
- Native mobile app
- Third-party integrations (Google Calendar, Apple Health)
- Advanced analytics dashboards
- Payments/subscriptions
- Full AI memory system (Phase 2)

---

## 9) Phases

**Phase 1 (Current — MVP)**
Core features: Auth, Tasks, Habits, Basic AI Plan, Notes, Calendar shell

**Phase 2 (Post-MVP)**
AI memory system, Journal analysis, Accountability tracker, Proactive nudges

**Phase 3 (Growth)**
Mobile app, integrations, social accountability, gamification (XP, streaks, levels)

---

## 10) Success Metrics

- User reaches a usable Today plan within 60 seconds of signup
- User completes at least 1 task + 1 habit in first session
- User returns the next day (Day 1 retention)
- User engages with AI feature at least once per session (Phase 2)
