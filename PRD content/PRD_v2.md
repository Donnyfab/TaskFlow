# Taskflow PRD (v2.0)

_The central hub for your entire life — powered by AI._

---

## 1) One-Liner

Taskflow is an AI-powered personal life operating system that connects every area of your life — tasks, habits, fitness, finance, nutrition, and more — into one intelligent hub that learns your behavior and helps you improve every day.

---

## 2) The Real Problem

Modern life is fragmented. People who want to take control of their health, productivity, and finances are forced to spread themselves across 6, 8, sometimes 10 different apps — none of which talk to each other.

- A task app that doesn't know you skipped the gym
- A habit tracker that doesn't know you're over budget
- A fitness app that doesn't know you haven't slept well
- A meal planner that doesn't know your schedule
- A journal that doesn't connect to anything
- Generic AI tools with zero context about who you actually are

The result: data everywhere, insight nowhere. People are busy tracking their lives without actually improving them.

**No platform currently connects the dots across all of these domains and uses that full picture to actively guide someone toward a better life.**

---

## 3) The Solution

Taskflow is the hub that ties everything together.

At its core, Taskflow handles the daily foundation:

- **Tasks** — daily planning, goals, to-dos
- **Habits** — build and track positive routines
- **Accountability** — custom streak trackers, resets, and reflection
- **Notes & Journal** — capture thoughts, reflect, and spot patterns
- **Calendar** — plan your week and month

Over time, Taskflow expands through a **satellite app ecosystem** — standalone apps for fitness, finance, and nutrition that each solve a specific problem on their own, but feed their data directly into Taskflow.

Taskflow's AI sits at the center of all of it. It doesn't just read your tasks — it reads your whole life, connects what other apps can't, and tells you what's actually going on:

> _"Your task completion drops every week you miss workouts. You've missed three in a row."_
> _"You tend to overspend when you haven't journaled in a few days."_
> _"Your most productive days start with a logged meal and a completed morning habit."_

That cross-domain intelligence is what no single-category app can offer. That's the Taskflow difference.

---

## 4) Ecosystem Architecture

Taskflow operates as a **hub-and-satellite model**:

### 🏠 Taskflow (The Hub)
The central OS. Users open it every day. It displays the most relevant insights from all connected apps without overwhelming them. The AI brain lives here and reads across all data sources.

### 🛰️ Satellite Apps (Standalone + Connected)
Each satellite app is a **fully standalone product** with real utility on its own. They attract users through their specific category and naturally introduce those users to Taskflow as the connective layer.

| App | Standalone Value | What It Feeds into Taskflow |
|---|---|---|
| **Taskflow Lift** (Workout) | Log workouts, track sets/reps, follow guided programs | Workout frequency, energy levels, physical activity patterns |
| **Taskflow Eats** (Meal Planner) | AI-generated meal plans, grocery lists, macro tracking | Nutrition patterns, meal consistency, energy and mood correlation |
| **Taskflow Finance** (Finance Tracker) | Track income, spending, savings goals | Spending patterns, financial stress signals, budget vs. behavior |

### The Integration Moment
When a satellite app user connects to Taskflow, they unlock the insight layer — the AI can now correlate their fitness data with their productivity, their nutrition with their mood, their finances with their journaling tone. That moment is the product's most powerful experience and the primary reason users stay.

## 7) Core Features — Taskflow Hub

### 🏠 Dashboard (Home HQ)
- AI daily briefing: "Here's what matters today"
- Surface-level insights from all connected satellite apps
- Progress summary: streaks, tasks completed, active goals
- Quick actions: Add task, log habit, open journal
- Intentionally minimal — shows what matters, hides what doesn't

### ✅ Tasks
- Create tasks with title, due date, priority, notes
- AI suggests priorities based on patterns and energy data
- Daily task planning: "What should I focus on today?"
- Recurring tasks, subtasks, lists, categories

### 🔁 Habits
- Create and track daily/weekly habits
- Streak tracking with visual progress
- AI detects struggling habits and suggests adjustments
- Habit stacking suggestions ("After you work out, do X")

### 🚫 Accountability
- Dedicated accountability tracker separate from habits
- Day counter with streak and reset logging
- Reflection prompt on resets
- AI provides encouragement and pattern analysis over time
- Custom accountability challenges (screen time, junk food, etc.)

### 📓 Notes & Journal
- General notes (organized by folders/tags)
- Daily journal with optional AI prompt
- AI reads journal entries over time and surfaces patterns

### 📅 Calendar
- Weekly and monthly view
- Connect tasks, habits, and satellite app activity to specific dates
- AI suggests scheduling based on best work times and energy patterns

### 🤖 AI Brain (Core Differentiator)
The AI studies everything across Taskflow and all connected satellite apps:

- Task completion rates and timing
- Habit streaks and failures
- Journal entries and emotional tone
- Workout frequency and consistency
- Nutrition patterns
- Spending behavior

And uses it to:
- Generate a personalized daily plan ("Plan My Day")
- Surface cross-domain insights: "You complete more tasks on days you exercise"
- Proactive nudges: "You haven't journaled in 3 days — want to do a quick check-in?"
- Spot hidden patterns: "Your spending increases on weeks your sleep habits break down"
- Be a coach, not just a tool

## 8) Satellite App — Taskflow Lift (Workout)

**Why this ships first:** Fitness has massive organic discoverability, overlaps directly with the core Taskflow audience, and is the most natural bridge back to the hub through habit and consistency tracking.

### Core Features
- Create custom workout programs with exercises, sets, reps, and rest times
- Log workouts with guided instructions and progress tracking
- Track strength progress over time with visual charts
- AI generates personalized workout plans based on goals and history
- Guided workout mode: step-by-step exercise instructions with timers

### Integration with Taskflow Hub
- Workout data feeds directly into the AI brain
- Taskflow can surface: "You haven't worked out in 4 days — your task completion historically drops around day 5"
- Workout completions count as habit checkmarks inside Taskflow
- AI daily plan accounts for workout schedule when planning the day

## 10) Phases

### Phase 1 — Core MVP (Current)
Taskflow hub: Auth, Tasks, Habits, AI Plan, Notes, Calendar.
Goal: Ship a product users want to return to every day.

### Phase 2 — AI Depth
Full AI memory system, journal pattern analysis, accountability tracker, proactive nudges, cross-session learning.
Goal: Make the AI feel like it actually knows the user.

### Phase 3 — Mobile + Polish
Native mobile app (iOS first), performance optimization, push notifications, widgets.
Goal: Make Taskflow a daily-open app, not just a desktop tool.

### Phase 4 — Satellite Launch (Taskflow Lift)
Build and ship the standalone workout app. Integrate with Taskflow hub.
Goal: Expand the ecosystem, prove the satellite model works, grow through a new acquisition channel.

### Phase 5 — Ecosystem Expansion
Launch Taskflow Eats (meal planner) and Taskflow Finance (finance tracker). Deepen cross-app AI insights.
Goal: Become the default personal life OS for users who want everything connected.

### Phase 6 — Platform
Open integration layer, third-party app connections (Apple Health, Google Calendar, Spotify, etc.), advanced analytics dashboard, social accountability features.
Goal: Taskflow becomes the hub for any app that touches your daily life.
