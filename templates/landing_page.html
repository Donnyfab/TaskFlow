The gap

Planning isn't the problem. Following through is.

Most apps collect your intentions and call that productivity. They store your tasks, your goals, your someday-maybes. They feel like progress. But they don't ask what actually happened. They don't remember your misses. They just hold your list until you give up and start a new one.

  • × Tasks pile up because nothing has a real deadline attached to a real consequence.
  • × Planning feels productive, so it becomes the substitute for doing the work.
  • × You skip commitments without anyone — or anything — asking what happened.
  • × AI assistants give advice. They don't follow up after the deadline.
  • × Every week starts fresh. Last week's misses vanish into the void.

Coach

Commitments

Specific. Dated. Provable.

A commitment is not a wish. It has a deadline with date and time, a proof requirement, and a status: pending, kept, missed, or partial. "Soon" and "this week" don't make it through the door.

Low for small habits. High for visible output — link, file, screenshot, shipped artifact.
Proof

Evidence over intention.

After a commitment deadline, Forge follows up. You report kept, missed, or partial — and submit proof when the work produces a real output. Every proof becomes an entry in your Evidence Vault.

The vault is permanent. Your transformation, logged.

How Forge works

Four steps.
Repeated until discipline is built.

Step 01

Set a goal.

Tell Coach what you want to build. Skip the questionnaire — Coach asks one question at a time, remembers your answers, and shapes a mission around what actually matters to you.

Step 02

Break it into commitments.

The goal becomes a mission. The mission becomes commitments. Each commitment has a deadline, a proof requirement, and a definition of done another person could verify.

Step 03

Submit proof of progress.

After each deadline, Forge follows up. You report what happened — kept, missed, or partial — and submit proof: a link, a file, a reflection, or a shipped artifact. Evidence over intention.

Step 04

Track consistency. Build discipline.

Proof becomes your Evidence Vault. Misses update memory. Coach shapes the next commitment based on what you actually did. The loop repeats — sharper each time.
