Questions

Everything you need to know about TaskFlow and how it works.

How does AI help?

×

TaskFlow’s AI learns what matters to you and surfaces your top priorities each morning. It cuts through the noise so you see only what deserves your attention today.

Can I import my tasks?

×

Yes. We support imports from most popular tools. Our onboarding walks you through it in minutes.

Can I use it offline?

×

Yes. TaskFlow works offline and syncs when you're back online. Your work never gets lost, and you stay productive anywhere.

What if I need help?

×

Our support team is here. Email us anytime or check our help docs for quick answers.

What about my data?

×

Your data is yours. We encrypt everything and never sell your information. You control what you share and what stays private.

Can teams use TaskFlow?

×

TaskFlow is built for individuals right now. We’re exploring team features based on user feedback.

Is there a mobile app?

×

TaskFlow works beautifully on any device. Web, iOS, and Android all sync instantly so you're always current.

How do habits work?

×

Set a habit once. TaskFlow reminds you daily and tracks your streak. You see patterns and progress over time.

How much does it cost?

×

Start free with core features. Paid plans unlock AI guidance, advanced habits, and priority support.

Do you offer discounts?

×

Students and educators get special pricing. Contact us for details or check the pricing page.
