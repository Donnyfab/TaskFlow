The story

How TaskFlow came to be.

Early 2025
The problem became personal
Paying for Todoist, Habitica, Day One, and Covenant Eyes separately — and none of them talked to each other. Something had to change.
Mid 2025
First version built
A simple Flask app with tasks and habits. No design, no AI — just the foundation to prove the concept worked.
Late 2025
AI brain added
The Anthropic API got integrated. The AI started reading task completion rates, habit streaks, and journal entries — and the insights it generated were genuinely useful.
March 2026
TaskFlow goes live
tflow.live launched. 2,000+ people signed up in the first weeks. The accountability tracker — which nobody else offers for free — became the most used feature.
Now
Building Phase 2
AI memory system, deeper journal analysis, proactive nudges, and eventually — a mobile app. The best is still ahead.
