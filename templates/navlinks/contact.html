Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about TaskFlow

How does TaskFlow work? +

TaskFlow gives you a single Today page that shows your plan, priorities, and next actions. The AI learns your patterns and helps you focus on what matters most each day.

Can I customize my workspace? +

Absolutely. You can organize tasks, habits, and reflections in a way that fits your workflow. TaskFlow adapts to how you think.

Can I import my existing tasks? +

Yes. TaskFlow supports imports from most productivity tools so you don’t lose momentum when switching.

How does the AI help? +

The AI watches what you complete and what you skip, then suggests realistic priorities so you build momentum without burnout.

Is my data secure? +

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We take privacy seriously and never sell your information.

Does TaskFlow work offline? +

Yes. TaskFlow works offline and syncs automatically once you’re back online.

Is there a free plan? +

Yes. You can start free and upgrade anytime when you want deeper insights and smarter AI guidance.

Can teams use TaskFlow? +

TaskFlow is built for individuals today. Team features are coming based on user feedback.
