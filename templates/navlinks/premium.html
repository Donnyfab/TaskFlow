Objection handling

The questions people ask right before they upgrade.

Keep the page honest, simple, and reassuring. The goal is not to pressure users. The goal is to make the value clear enough that hesitation drops.

Is Premium actually worth it?+
Premium makes the most sense once you are already using TaskFlow consistently and want sharper guidance. If the free app helps you stay organized, Premium is what helps you spot patterns, protect focus, and improve faster.
What do I actually get when I upgrade?+
Depending on plan, Premium adds the AI life coach, weekly AI summaries, Focus Mode, consistency maps, advanced journal analysis, deeper accountability tools, and priority support.
Do I lose the free app if I never upgrade?+
No. TaskFlow's core experience stays free. Premium is an added layer for users who want deeper AI help and stronger review loops, not a paywall around the basics.
Can I cancel or change plans later?+
Yes. When a paid plan is active, you can switch or cancel it. If billing is not live for your account yet, you will see the full pricing terms before you are charged.
Is my data still private on Premium?+
Yes. Premium does not change the expectation that your data is yours. Journals, accountability data, habits, and planning information remain private by default under the same product privacy standards.
Who is Premium best for?+
Students, creators, founders, and anyone already doing the work but tired of repeating the same avoidable mistakes. Premium is strongest when you want the app to guide, not just store.
